Welcome to COVID Watch

A little about us...

Our website was founded on March 10, 2020 to help people better undersand the COVID-19 pandemic. We believe that it is critical to keep everyone informed during this challenging time. We are committed to supporting family, friends and the uOttawa community with our latest updates and information on COVID-19. We want to create an accessible platform for our students and general public with informative and educational news.

We inform people of the symptoms of COVID-19. We teach them how to protect themselves from being infected.

Our mission

Our mission is to share the latest news about COVID-19 and to educate the public on how to take appropriate health and safety precautions to further eliminate the spread of the virus.

We provide current news on the pandemic in Canada and around the globe. In addition, we closely monitor our data to ensure it's accurate and valid. We have developed newletters and regular updates for our users on the most recent travel regulations, Health Canada announcements and research regarding this matter.

Thank you for signing up to learn more about COVID-19. We will do our best to keep everyone informed. We wish you health and safety.

How we can help...

Please take TWO minutes to fill in your information and register as a user. We will keep you updated with the latest news in the community and around the globe.
