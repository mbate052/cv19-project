Welcome to COVID Watch

A little about us...

Our website was found on March 10, 2020 to help people better response to COVID-19.We believe it is critical to keep everyone informed during this challenging time. We are committed to support family, friends and the uOttawa community to stay healthy.We want to create an accessible platform for our students and general public with informative and educational news.

We deliver and inform people of the symptons of COVI-19. We teach them how to protect themselves from being infected.

Our mission

Our mission is to share the latest news about COVID-19, educate public about healthy and safty protcols and reduce the spread of the virus.

We provide the current news on the pandemic in Canada and around the globe. In addition, we closely monitor our data to ensure it's accurate and valid. We have developped newletters and regular updates to our users on the most recent update on status, travelling regulations, HealthCanada announcement and research regarding to this matter.

Thank you for signing up to learn more abour COVID-19. We will do our best to keep everyone informed. We wish you health and safety.

How we can help...

Please take TWO minutes to fill in your information and register as a user. We will keep you updated with the latest news in the community and around the globe.
