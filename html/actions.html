How to Protect Yourself and Your Families


Self-Protection

Cleaning

  • Clean and disinfect frequently touched items daily
  • Disinfect anything you brought from the outside of your home using 90% alcohol
  • Regular cleaning can also help you stay organized and positive throughout this time
  • Keep in mind that the virus can live up to two weeks. Therefore, sanitize your parcels and packages if needed

Protecting your Community

  • It's important to understand that this virus could bring potential risks to people around you, espically the elderly and people with underlying conditions
  • Respect others. Practice social distancing religiously and avoid close contact with high risk individuals
  • ALWAYS keep your mask on for your own safety and for the safety for others
  • Be honest. You should inform anyone with whom you had close contact if you test positive
  • Help each other. While close contact is discouraged, you can always help out people who are in need with simple gestures such as dropping off groceries or greeting people over the phone. This will help people relieve their mental stress
