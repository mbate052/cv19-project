Watch for symptoms

If you have any of the following symptoms, it's best to stay home. If symptoms persist, get tested for COVID-19.

  • Fever or chills
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • Fatigue
  • Muscle or body aches
  • Headache
  • New loss of taste or smell
  • Sore throat
  • Congestion or runny nose
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Diarrhea

You may experience different symptoms based on your medical background. Ask your doctor for more information.

Emergency Symptoms

Be on the lookout for these signs. If you experience any, call 911.

  • Trouble breathing
  • Persistent pain or pressure in the chest
  • New confusion
  • Inability to wake or stay awake
  • Bluish lips or face
